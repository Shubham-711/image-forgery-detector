About the Project

This tool demonstrates a deep learning approach (U-Net with DCT analysis) to detect common image manipulations like copy-move and splicing forgeries.

How It Works

  • Grayscale & Resize
  • Block-based DCT Analysis
  • U-Net Segmentation Model
  • Thresholding ({{ FORGERY_THRESHOLD * 100 }}%) for Classification

Limitations

  • May miss subtle or novel forgery types.
  • Sensitive to image quality/compression.
